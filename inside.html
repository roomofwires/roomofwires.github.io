INSIDE

Delve into their isolated approach

We are an electronic music duo from the UK. We use synthesizers, computers, field recordings and handcrafted software to create twisted, fragile yet beautiful sounds. Since forming in 2013, we have released several EPs and two LPs: White Transit - "An album of epic intensity, very powerful chilled electronics" and Test Subject - "Amazing atmospheric and dark sonic landscapes", as well as countless contributions to compilations and film scores for "Shifter" and "Versus". We've never met, preferring to work in isolation, sharing ideas and sounds over the internet, one of us using hardware and a tangle of physical wires, the other lost in software and virtual cables.

To find out more, follow us on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram, and join our Mailing List.

You can listen to us on Bandcamp and SoundCloud.
